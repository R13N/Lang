# Precedences overview

Precedence | Operators | Associativity
---------- | --------- | -------------
1 | ``:`` | right
2 | ``&&``, ``||`` | left
3 | ``==``, ``!=`` | left
4 | ``+``, ``-`` | left
5 | ``!`` | prefix
6 | ``*``, ``/``, ``%`` | left
7 | ``²``, ``³`` | postfix
